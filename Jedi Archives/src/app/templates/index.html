The Legacy of the Jedi

For over a thousand generations, the Jedi Knights were the guardians of peace and justice in the Old Republic. Before the dark times... before the Empire...

The Force: An Energy Field Created by All Living Things

The Force is what gives a Jedi his power. It's an energy field created by all living things. It surrounds us, penetrates us, and binds the galaxy together.

The Sith Code

Known for their ruthlessness, the Sith abide by their code, which states: "Peace is a lie, there is only passion. Through passion, I gain strength. Through strength, I gain power. Through power, I gain victory. Through victory, my chains are broken."

The Jedi Order

The Jedi Order was all about maintaining peace and order in the galaxy. They were diplomats and warriors, serving as guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic for thousands of years.

Lightsabers: The Weapon of the Jedi

A symbol of a Jedi's connection to the Force, a lightsaber is both a weapon and a tool. The construction of a lightsaber is a rite of passage, with the color of the kyber crystal reflecting its wielder's personality and position.
